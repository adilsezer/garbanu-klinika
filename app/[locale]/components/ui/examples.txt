/components/ui
UI components are more specific than common components and are typically used to build the unique parts of your interface. They are often specific to your application's context.

ProductCard.tsx: A card component displaying a product, used in product lists.
UserProfile.tsx: A component to display user profile information.
Accordion.tsx: For collapsible/expandable content sections.
Tabs.tsx: Tabbed interface components for sectioning content.
Carousel.tsx: A carousel or slider component, maybe for image galleries.
RatingStar.tsx: A component for displaying and possibly selecting star ratings.
Timeline.tsx: A component for displaying events or steps in a timeline format
